Yellowstone

A Breathtaking National Park

Located in the U.S. state of Wyoming, Yellowstone was the first National Park in the United States. Yellowstone spans over an area of 3,471 square miles. At Yellowstone, you can expect to see geysers, hot springs, and boiling mud pots. It offers over 2,000 campsites, but the main attraction is the world renowned Old Faithful.
