Bora Bora

About Bora Bora

A small island located in the South Pacific, Bora Bora lies northwest of Tahiti in the French Polynesia. Its total land area spans roughly 12 square miles. The island's lagoons, sands, and sunsets set the scene for the perfect romantic getaway. Swim with fish, view the stunning Mount Otemanu, or watch shark feedings. This island truly offers a relaxing getaway.

Population: 10,605
Average Vacation Cost: $3,184
Average Tourist Arrivals: ~300,000 (French Polynesia)
