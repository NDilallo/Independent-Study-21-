San Miguel de Allende

A City in the Hills

San Miguel de Allende is a colonial city located in Mexico. The city is known for its thriving art scene and cultural festivals. The nearby Templo de San Francisco church is popular for its 18th century design. This city is perfect for inspiring artists due to its spectacular color and light.

Population: 174,615
Average Vacation Cost: $1,273
Average Tourist Arrivals: ~1,600,000
