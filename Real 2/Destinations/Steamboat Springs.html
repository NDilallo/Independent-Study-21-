Steamboat Springs

About Steamboat Springs

Steamboat Springs is a municipality in northern Colorado's Yampa Valley. This city is where skiing first flourished in the U.S. After a long day of activity, you can relax in one of the many geothermal hotsprings.

Population: 12,928
Average Vacation Cost: $1,300
Average Tourist Arrivals: ~100,000
