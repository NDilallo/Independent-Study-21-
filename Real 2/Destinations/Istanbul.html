Istanbul

A Culmination of Cultures

Located in the northeast sector, Istanbul stands as the capital of Turkey. This city reflects the cultural influences of all of the empires that once ruled it. The holy sites Sultanhmet and Beyoğlu are just one of many attractions. This place feels like many cities within one.

Population: 15,460,000
Area: 2,063 square miles
Average Tourist Arrivals: >2,700,000
