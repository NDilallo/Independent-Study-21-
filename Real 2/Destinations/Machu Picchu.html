Machu Picchu

A Piece of Ancient Architecture

Located in the South American country of Peru, Machu Picchu is Peru's most-visited site. Dating all the way back to the mid 15th century, this piece of architecture sits atop a plateau within the Amazonian jungle. The multiple-day hiking trip is worth the reward that the ruins provide at the top!

Elevation: 7,972 feet
Average Vacation Cost: $2,233
Average Tourist Arrivals: >1,500,000
