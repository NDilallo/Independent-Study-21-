Reinebringen

A Breathtaking Hike

Located in the municipality of Moskenes, Norway, Reinebringen is one of the most popular hiking trails. Surrounded by miles of mountains and water, this hike is one you'll never forget. While you're there, you can also explore the wondrous country of Norway!

Hike Duration: 1-2 hours
Difficulty: Medium
Distance: 2km
