About St. Moritz

St. Moritz is a luxury resort town located in Switzerland. This resort overlooks a wide open glaciated valley. Hundreds of miles of ski trails and the frozen lakes serve as popular sports venues.

Population: 5,233
Average Vacation Cost: $1,943
Average Tourist Arrivals: >500,000
