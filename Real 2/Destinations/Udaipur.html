Udaipur

A Group of Breathtaking Islands

Known as the Venice of the East, Udaipur is a large city in India. Established in 1559, the city is surrounded by a series of artificial lakes. It is known for its royal residences, stunning courtyards, and impressive paintings. City Palace is a top monument.

Population: 451,100
Elevation: 1,388 feet
Average Tourist Arrivals: >1,400,000
